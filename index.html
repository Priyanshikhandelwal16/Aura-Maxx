Aura Maxx Wall Putty

Learn More

The Foundation of Strong & Flawless Walls

Aura Maxx Wall Putty is a premium, cement-based wall preparation solution engineered to deliver smooth, durable, and long-lasting surfaces. Designed for both interior and exterior applications, it creates the perfect base for superior paint finishes while enhancing the overall strength of walls.

Formulated with high-quality minerals and advanced bonding technology, Aura Maxx Wall Putty offers excellent adhesion on plaster, concrete, and precast surfaces. Its ultra-smooth finish minimizes paint consumption while ensuring brighter, more uniform, and long-lasting colours.

Aura Maxx Wall Putty offers enhanced performance benefits:

  1. Ultra-Smooth Finish: Provides a fine, even surface that enhances the final paint appearance.
  2. Superior Bond Strength: Ensures excellent adhesion to plaster, concrete, and precast walls.
  3. Water & Damp Resistance: Protects walls from moisture penetration and surface damage.
  4. Crack-Resistant Durability: Minimizes shrinkage and cracking for long-lasting structural integrity.
  5. Optimized Paint Consumption: Reduces paint absorption, delivering richer colour output with less paint.
